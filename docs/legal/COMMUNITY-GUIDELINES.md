# AURA Community Guidelines

> Initial operational draft — requires review by qualified legal counsel before public launch.

No scams, phishing, wallet-draining links, fraudulent asset claims, impersonation, or manipulation. Never solicit seed phrases, private keys, wallet permissions, signatures, or transaction approvals.

No harassment, credible threats, hateful conduct, sexual exploitation, child sexual abuse material, non-consensual intimate material, doxxing, or glorification of serious violence. Graphic content may be restricted even in documentary contexts.

No spam, malicious automation, artificial engagement, repetitive promotion, enforcement evasion, compromised accounts, or misleading identity practices. Credit creators and respect copyright and other rights.

AURA may reduce visibility, remove content, limit features, suspend or disable accounts, preserve evidence, and report emergencies or illegal content. Severity, context, history, and risk inform action. Appeals: **[APPEALS CONTACT PLACEHOLDER]**.
